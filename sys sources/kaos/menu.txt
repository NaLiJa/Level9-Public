$1    A. Upper case alpha that user will type
$1   BB. Screen mode. One of 01, 03, 06, 07, 14 or 16.
$1    C. 0 for text-only modes,
$1       1 to allow PICTURES without loading screen,
$1       2 to allow PICTURES with loading screen.
$1    D. 0 for legal,
$1       1 for fast MGA/not-so-slow EGA,
$1       2 for normal EGA,
$1       3 for fast EGA,
$1       4 for screen-switch between text-only/CGA low-res.
$1       5 for screen-switch on EGA card CGA monitor
$1       6 for screen-switch on EGA card EGA monitor
$1   EE. Loading screen picture number (00=no title).

$1    F. Board type
$1    G. Monitor type (0=any)
$1    H. Prefered width/characters (1=40, 0=any)
$1    I. Ascii screen mode to suggest.

$1 $. - Remove "$1 " and put "$." is column 1 to display the 
$1 board type (F) and monitor type (G) in your PC system.
